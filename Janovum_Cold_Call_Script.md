# JANOVUM — COLD CALL PITCH SCRIPT

---

## INTRO

"Hey, how are you doing? This is Jaden with Janovum — am I speaking with [name]?"

*(If they say yes, keep it moving. Don't overthink the intro.)*

---

## HOOK

"Yeah so basically, I'm not sure if you've heard about it yet, but a lot of businesses have been bringing in AI to handle things they used to pay people for — answering phones, booking appointments, following up with customers, running their whole front desk. It's been picking up like crazy and I just wanted to get you ahead of the curve before your competitors do."

---

## THE QUESTION

"But real quick — is there anything in your business right now that's either taking up way too much of your time, costing too much, or you just wish ran smoother? Could be missed calls, scheduling headaches, following up with leads, anything like that."

*(Let them talk. Don't interrupt. Whatever they say, that's your in.)*

---

## THE OFFER (after they answer)

"Yeah, so that's literally what we do. We set up AI-powered systems for businesses — so for example, we have an AI receptionist that answers your phone 24/7, sounds just like a real person, books appointments, answers customer questions, and never misses a call. On top of that we do email automation, lead follow-up, websites, dashboards — basically whatever's slowing you down, we build something that handles it automatically."

---

## THE VALUE

"And here's the thing — a full-time receptionist costs you what, $30K-$40K a year minimum? Our AI does the same job, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, never calls in sick, never takes a lunch break — and it's $500 a month. That's it. Plus you get a full dashboard where you can see every call, every appointment, everything in real time."

---

## KEEP IT REAL

"And I'm not gonna sit here and act like we come in and change everything overnight — but the stuff that's eating up your time and money for no reason? The missed calls, the leads that fall through the cracks, the phone ringing while you're with a customer? That's what we take off your plate."

---

## THE CLOSE

"Look, I don't wanna take up too much of your time — but I can show you exactly how it works in like 15 minutes on a quick Google Meet call. It's way easier to see it than hear me explain it. When's a good time for you this week?"

*(If they hesitate)*: "No pressure at all — I can send you a quick link with all the info so you can check it out on your own time. What's a good email?"

*(If they give email)*: Send them the FAQ page link — **janovum.com/faq** — and follow up in 2 days.

---

## IF THEY ASK ABOUT PRICE

"So it's $1,000 to get everything set up — that covers your AI receptionist, your dedicated phone number, the full dashboard, everything trained on your business. And then it's $500 a month after that. No hidden fees, no per-minute charges, no long-term contracts. And if you ever wanna cancel, it's just 30 days notice."

---

## IF THEY SAY "I ALREADY HAVE A RECEPTIONIST"

"That's perfect actually — our AI handles everything your receptionist can't. After hours, weekends, when they're on lunch, when the phone's ringing off the hook and they're already on a call. Think of it as backup that never misses. A lot of our clients use both."

---

## IF THEY SAY "I DON'T TRUST AI"

"I totally get that. But honestly, the people calling your business won't even know it's AI — it sounds completely natural, like a real person at the front desk. And you can listen to every single call from your dashboard. If you don't like something it said, we change it same day. You're always in control."

---

## IF THEY SAY "I'LL THINK ABOUT IT"

"For sure, no rush at all. Let me send you a link so you can see what we do — and if it makes sense down the road, I'm here. What's the best email for you?"

*(Send janovum.com/faq + follow up in 3-5 days)*

---

## IF THEY SAY NO

"All good, I appreciate your time. If anything ever changes, you've got my number. Have a good one."

*(Don't push it. Some people come back later.)*

---

- **Talk like a person, not a salesman.** If it sounds like a script, they'll tune out.
- **Match their energy.** If they're chill, be chill. If they're in a rush, get to the point faster.
- **The goal isn't to close on the phone** — it's to get the demo scheduled on Google Meet. That's it.
- **After the demo, send the contract immediately** — use the contract sender tool so they can sign before the call even ends.
- **Always send the FAQ link** (janovum.com/faq) to anyone who says "send me more info."
- **Follow-up schedule:** If they gave you their email but didn't book, follow up Day 2, Day 5, and Day 10. After that, move on.
