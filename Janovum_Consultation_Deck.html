What This Actually Costs the Traditional Way

Let's be real about what it costs to handle all of this without automation. This isn't exaggerated — this is what most small businesses are actually spending, even if they don't think of it this way.

What businesses typically spend:

Part-time receptionist (20-30 hrs/week) $2,000-3,000/mo
After-hours answering service $300-800/mo
Scheduling, CRM, and email tools $100-300/mo
Total monthly range
$2,500 - $4,000/mo

And even with all of that, you're still dealing with:

Nobody answering after hours, weekends, or holidays — customers call your competitor instead

One person can only take one call at a time — busy signal means lost business

No automated follow-ups — leads fall through the cracks because nobody follows up

Employee turnover — someone quits and you're back to square one, retraining
