FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need any tech or coding skills? ›
None. The entire toolkit is pre-built and ready to deploy. If you can send an email and make a phone call, you can run a Janovum agency.
What exactly do I sell to local businesses? ›
AI receptionist (24/7 phone answering), appointment booking, lead follow-up, and AI-powered customer engagement. Any local business — restaurant, salon, auto shop, dental office — can benefit immediately.
How does the 15% revenue share work? ›
When you charge a client $500/month, you log that deal in the Operator Hub. Janovum's share is $75 (15%). You keep $425 (85%). Once Stripe is connected, this happens automatically. For now, you track it manually in the Revenue tab.
What's included in my monthly subscription? ›
Full access to the 33-tab Janovum toolkit: CRM, AI receptionist, lead generator, sales scripts, proposal templates, contract signing, revenue tracker, proactive AI agent, and white-label branding tools.
How fast can I sign my first client? ›
Most operators sign their first client within the first week. The toolkit includes 100+ pre-loaded local business leads and industry-specific sales pitches so you're ready to call the same day you sign up.
Can I cancel anytime? ›
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime. Your clients and their data remain yours.
