💡 General
What is Janovum? ▼

Janovum is an AI-powered business automation company. We provide intelligent tools that handle your phone calls, customer inquiries, email follow-ups, appointment scheduling, and more — all running 24/7 in the cloud without you lifting a finger. Think of us as your always-on digital team.

How does it work? ▼

We set up AI systems custom-trained on your business — your services, pricing, hours, and FAQs. Once configured, the AI handles customer interactions automatically. Calls get answered, appointments get booked, leads get followed up on, and you see everything in a real-time dashboard. It runs in the cloud so there's nothing to install on your end.

What does the AI actually do? ▼

The AI can:

  • Answer your business phone line 24/7 with natural, human-like conversation
  • Book appointments directly into your calendar
  • Answer customer questions about your services, pricing, and hours
  • Route urgent calls to you or your team
  • Send automated follow-up emails to leads
  • Run email campaigns and respond to inquiries
  • Track everything in a dashboard you can access anytime
Do I need to do anything to keep it running? ▼

No. Everything runs on its own in the cloud. Once we set it up, the AI handles calls, books appointments, and follows up with customers automatically. You just check your dashboard whenever you want to see how things are going. If your business info changes (new hours, new services, etc.), just let us know and we'll update the AI.

What types of businesses does Janovum work for? ▼

Any business that gets phone calls or customer inquiries. We work with real estate agents, restaurants, medical offices, law firms, home service companies (plumbing, HVAC, electrical), salons, auto shops, and more. If your business talks to customers, we can automate it.

📞 AI Receptionist
How does the AI Receptionist work? ▼

When someone calls your business number, the AI picks up instantly. It sounds natural — like a real person at the front desk. It listens to what the caller needs, gives accurate answers based on your business info, and can book appointments, take messages, or route the call to you. It works 24/7, including nights, weekends, and holidays.

Do I get my own phone number? ▼

Yes! You get a dedicated business phone number. You can forward your existing number to it, or use the new number directly on your website, business cards, and ads. Callers will never know they're talking to AI — it sounds completely natural.

Will callers know it's AI? ▼

No. The AI uses advanced voice technology that sounds natural and human. It uses casual language, contractions, and conversational tone — just like a real person picking up the phone. Most callers have no idea they're talking to AI.

What if I already have a receptionist? ▼

Our AI handles the calls your receptionist can't — after hours, weekends, lunch breaks, high-volume periods, or when they're busy with another caller. Think of it as backup that never calls in sick. Many clients use both and let the AI pick up the overflow.

Can the AI transfer calls to me? ▼

Yes. The AI can be configured to route urgent or specific calls directly to you or a team member. You set the rules — certain keywords, VIP callers, or emergency situations can trigger an immediate transfer.

Can the AI book appointments? ▼

Yes. The AI checks your available time slots and books appointments directly during the call. The caller picks a day and time, the AI confirms it, and you see it instantly in your dashboard. No double-bookings — the AI knows what's already scheduled.

Can I listen to the calls? ▼

Yes. Every call is logged in your dashboard with the full transcript and recording. You can review any call, see exactly what was said, and make sure the AI is handling things the way you want.
